# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

# Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn and retention behavior in a subscription-based business environment. The objective is to identify churn patterns, retention drivers, and customer lifetime trends to support better business decisions.

# Business Problem
Customer churn directly affects recurring revenue and growth in subscription businesses. This project aims to uncover why customers leave and what factors are associated with long-term retention.

# Objectives
   Measure overall churn and retention rates
   Identify customer segments most likely to churn
   Analyze tenure and customer lifetime patterns
   Explore retention drivers
   Provide practical recommendations to reduce churn

# Tools Used
   Excel
   Power BI
   Python 

# Key Insights
   Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rates
   Customers with short tenure were most likely to leave
   Certain payment methods and pricing segments had higher churn risk
   Long-tenure customers showed significantly stronger retention

# Recommendations
   Improve onboarding and first-year engagement
   Incentivize customers to move to longer-term contracts
   Build targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers
   Monitor early churn indicators proactively
